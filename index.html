About Me

Ling Do: Personal Trainer

My name is Ling Do and I am the founder of Do Fitness. When I was 13 years old, I initially developed an interest in exercise. I began by performing numerous calisthenics. I began bodybuilding in my 20s because I was so fascinated by how drastically my body might alter depending on my diet and a certain exercise regimen. I have a strong enthusiasm for fitness and am constantly looking for new, creative, and enjoyable activities to help me expand my muscles as much as possible and lose body fat. I'm committed to learning and creating a better, more enjoyable manner to get the outcomes we want.

Initially, I trained my family and friends as a form of recreation when we would work out at the gym. I swiftly came to the conclusion that I like encouraging and holding my family and friends accountable for achieving their health goals. This ignited my desire to become a personal trainer.

I want to share the experience of one of my very first clients who helped me decide to become a trainer. I still recall the Friday afternoon in 2016 when one of my first clients, who I had been working out with three times a week for about eight months, called me and said, "Hey, I got some news from my doctor." He will discontinue my type 2 diabetic medication based on the results of my physical exam test. He also became more physically stronger and had more energy during the course of our eight months of training. When he doubted himself, my client praised me for encouraging him, keeping him responsible, and having faith in him. Fitness is a lifestyle and it starts from within.

Ling Do

Founder & CEO
